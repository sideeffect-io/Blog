Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Funny Boum is designed to respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Data Collection

Funny Boum does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No account creation
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No personal information of any kind

On-Device Data

Any game data (such as settings or progress) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to the developer.

Third-Party Services

Funny Boum uses TelemetryDeck to collect some anonymous analytics like information about the type of grid you are playing.

Children’s Privacy

Because no personal data is collected, Funny Boum does not knowingly collect information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
